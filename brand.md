# Brand Guidelines

## Visual Concept
The sweepstake website should feel premium, engaging, and modern. We are opting for a clean, vibrant aesthetic with "glassmorphism" elements.

## Color Palette
*   **Primary Background:** Deep Obsidian `#0B0C10` with subtle radial gradients `#1F2833`.
*   **Card Background:** Semi-transparent white or slightly tinted glass `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)` with blur `backdrop-filter: blur(10px)`.
*   **Accent Color:** Vibrant Cyan `#66FCF1` to `#45A29E` for highlights and active states.
*   **Text Primary:** Pure White `#FFFFFF` for maximum contrast.
*   **Text Secondary:** Light Grey `#C5C6C7`.

## Typography
*   **Font Family:** `Outfit`, sans-serif (imported via Google Fonts).
*   **Headers:** Bold, clean, slight letter spacing.
*   **Body:** Regular, highly legible.

## Micro-Animations
*   Cards should scale up slightly on hover `transform: translateY(-5px) scale(1.02)`.
*   Smooth transitions for all interactive elements `transition: all 0.3s ease-in-out`.
